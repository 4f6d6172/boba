About Koalas

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia. They are known for their large noses, fluffy ears, and love of eucalyptus leaves. Although often called "koala bears," they are not bears at all!

Koala Habitat

Koalas live in eucalyptus forests and woodlands. They spend most of their time in trees, sleeping up to 20 hours a day! Their sharp claws help them grip tree bark and climb with ease.

Fun Facts

  • Koalas have fingerprints almost identical to humans!
  • They eat around 500g of eucalyptus leaves a day.
  • Baby koalas are called "joeys."
